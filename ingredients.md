# Ingredients for Butter Paneer

The ingredients required for making Butter Paneer is :

- Paneer
- Oil
- Water
- Onions
- Ripe tomatoes
- Butter
- Diary Cream (cooking cream/fresh cream/whipped cream)
- Ginger garlic paste
- Garam masala
- Red chilli powder
- Spices (cardamoms, clove,cinnamon, coriander, bay leaf, etc.)
- Cashew/Almond (or any other suitable nuts)
